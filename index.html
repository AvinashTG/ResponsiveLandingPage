Watermelon

Watermelon is a refreshing and hydrating fruit, perfect for hot summer days. It's composed of about 92% water, making it an excellent choice for staying hydrated. Rich in vitamins A and C, watermelon also provides a good amount of antioxidants, which can help combat free radicals in the body. Its sweet, juicy flesh is not only delicious but also low in calories, making it a healthy snack option.

Guava

Guava is a tropical fruit known for its unique flavor and numerous health benefits. Rich in vitamin C, one guava provides more than double the daily recommended intake, boosting immunity and skin health. Its high dietary fiber content aids in digestion and helps maintain a healthy gut. Guava also contains antioxidants like lycopene and beta-carotene, which can help reduce the risk of chronic diseases.
